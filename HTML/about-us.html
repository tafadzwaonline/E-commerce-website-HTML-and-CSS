About Kahwai Shoes

Get directions

Kahwai Shoes was started back in 2017 by Tafadzwa Kahwai, The team started producing shoes only in 2019. In addition to female and male, there is a children's line. Shoes of non-dull styles and rich colors immediately catch the eye.

We are located in Harare, Zimbabwe along Pennywise Samora Machael.
Being an official Axecure Sneakers partner, we sell their shoes at convenient prices. We focus on team-designed collaborations that appeal to our global community of global independents, creatives, and free-thinkers.

Mission

Our mission is what drives us to do everything possible to expand human potential. We do that by creating groundbreaking sport innovations, by making our products more sustainably, by building a creative and diverse global team and by making a positive impact in communities where we live and work. We strive to keep our work unique and smart, with room for a little magic.
